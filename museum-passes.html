🎟️ Free Museum Passes

Two great programs let your family visit world-class museums for free. Here's everything you need to know.

📚 Museum Adventure Pass

Free museum admission — check out a pass from your Lake County library

The Museum Adventure Pass program lets any Lake County library cardholder borrow a pass and get free or reduced admission to dozens of museums and cultural attractions. Each pass covers the whole family.

museumadventure.org →

How it works
  1. Find a pass in your library's catalog
  2. Check it out (3–7 day loan)
  3. Present at the museum
  4. Whole family gets in free

🏦 Bank of America Museums on Us

Free general admission the first full weekend of every month

Bank of America and Merrill Lynch cardholders get free general admission to 225+ museums and cultural attractions on the first full Saturday and Sunday of each month. Works at many of the same great Chicago and Milwaukee venues.

bankofamerica.com/museums-on-us →

How it works
  1. First full weekend = Sat + Sun
  2. Show BofA/Merrill card + photo ID
  3. Admission is for cardholder only
  4. General admission only
